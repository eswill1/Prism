# Prism Masthead Directions
### Version 0.1

---

## Goal

Design three distinct masthead/logo directions for Prism and choose one as the live default.

---

## Directions

### 1. Wire

- Publication-first
- Best fit for a serious national news front door
- Full `The Prism Wire` wordmark carries the brand
- Mark is present, but subordinate to the masthead

Why it works:

- feels like a real newsroom masthead
- supports trust and habit
- lets Prism compete in the visual language of established news sites without becoming generic

### 2. Signal

- Product-forward
- Keeps the prism metaphor highly visible
- Best fit if the brand should feel more analytical and next-generation

Why it works:

- more distinctive as a technology-informed news product
- stronger metaphor signal

Risk:

- can drift toward product branding over publication branding

### 3. Ledger

- Institutional and archival
- Strong public-utility energy
- Best fit if Prism wants to feel civic, durable, and almost library-like

Why it works:

- high trust
- stable and sober

Risk:

- may feel too static or bureaucratic for a daily news product

---

## Chosen Direction

### `Signal`

`Signal` is now the live default in the site header.

Reason:

- it is the most distinctive of the three without feeling unserious
- it keeps the prism metaphor visible at the brand level
- it helps Prism feel like a next-generation news product rather than a generic publication clone

---

- Live header uses `Wire` via the shared masthead component.
